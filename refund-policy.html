Refund Policy

At Tele Global Pay, we are committed to providing excellent bill negotiation services to our clients. We understand that sometimes circumstances may change, and you may need to request a refund. This policy outlines our guidelines for refund requests.


30-Day Refund Guarantee.

We offer a 30-day refund guarantee for our bill negotiation services. If you are not satisfied with the results of our services or have a valid reason for requesting a refund, you can do so within 30 days from the date of your initial payment.


Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, you must meet the following criteria:

  • 1. Your refund request must be submitted within 30 days from the date of your initial payment.
  • 2. You must have fully cooperated with our team, providing all necessary information and documents required for the bill negotiation process.
  • 3. Your refund request must be based on valid reasons such as dissatisfaction with the service, failure to achieve negotiated savings, or any other legitimate concern related to our service.

Refund Process.

To request a refund, please contact our customer support team at refund@teleglobalpay.com. Your request should include the following information:

  • 1. Your full name and contact information.
  • 2. Details of your bill negotiation project, including the date of payment.
  • 3. The reason for your refund request, including any relevant supporting documents or evidence.

Once we receive your request, we will review it and respond within 2 business working days to confirm whether your refund request is approved.


Refund Options.

If your refund request is approved, we will process the refund as follows:

  • 1. If you paid by credit card, the refund will be issued to the same credit card used for the initial payment.
  • 2. If you paid by other methods, we will work with you to determine the most suitable refund method.

Exceptions.

Note that this refund policy does not cover any fees or costs associated with third-party services, such as government fees or expenses related to telecom services. Additionally, any services provided after the 30-day period are not eligible for a refund.

Please note that this refund policy is subject to change, and any updates will be posted on our website.
