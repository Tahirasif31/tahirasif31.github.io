So, we thought to make this problem easier for consumers and provide a smooth way.

Privacy Policy

We believe privacy matters. We want to get paid for saving you money, not for selling your data. We developed this policy for you to understand how we collect, use, communicate, and disclose and make use of personal information. The following outlines our privacy policy:

Prior or during the process of gathering personal information, we will clearly outline the reasons for which we are collecting this information.

We will collect and utilize personal information exclusively with the purpose of fulfilling the specific objectives that we have defined, along with any other compatible purposes, unless we have received your written consent or are mandated to do so by law.

We will retain personal information only for the duration necessary to meet these stated objectives.

Our collection of personal information will adhere to lawful and equitable methods, and, when appropriate, will be carried out with the knowledge or consent of the individuals concerned.

Personal data should be pertinent to the intended purposes of use, and, to the extent required for these purposes, it should be accurate, comprehensive, and kept up-to-date.

We will implement reasonable security measures to safeguard personal information against loss, theft, unauthorized access, disclosure, duplication, usage, or alteration.

We are ready and willing to assist in deleting your personal information upon request whenever it is feasible. If you wish to have your personal data removed, please feel free to contact us via email at privacy@teleglobalpay.com.

We will make information about our policies and procedures concerning the management of personal information readily available to Tele Global Pay's customers.

Furthermore, we may authorize certain third-party companies to assist us in customizing advertisements that we believe may be of interest to users. These companies may also gather and employ other data related to user activities on our Sites and/or Services, such as tailoring ads on third-party services. These entities may deliver advertisements that could also set cookies and track user behavior. To opt out of personal information affecting the delivery of Google AdWords, please visit https://www.google.com/ads/preferences/.

Our commitment is to conduct our business in alignment with these principles, ensuring the preservation and protection of the confidentiality of personal information."
