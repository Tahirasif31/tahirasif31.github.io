About us

Three years ago, we started Tele Global Pay to help consumers negotiate their bills because sometimes corporations would incorrectly charge them, and consumers didn't even know what service they were paying for.

When consumers contacted the corporation, they often had frustratingly long calls, and we didn't believe that the way corporations treated consumers was fair.


So, we thought to make this problem easier for consumers and provide a smooth way.
